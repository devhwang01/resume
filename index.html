Professional Summary

Visionary rapper, songwriter, actor, and entrepreneur with over three decades of influence in the entertainment industry. Known for pioneering contributions to hip-hop, an iconic laid-back style, and successful ventures spanning music, film, business, and philanthropy. Adept at brand partnerships, creative direction, and audience engagement.

Work Experience

Hip-Hop Artist & Performer

1992 - Present

  • Released over 19 studio albums, including Doggystyle, Tha Doggfather, and Doggumentary, selling millions worldwide.
  • Collaborated with top artists such as Dr. Dre, Tupac, Pharrell, and Eminem.
  • Multiple Grammy nominations and chart-topping hits spanning decades.
  • Continually evolved sound and style while maintaining cultural relevance.

Entrepreneur & Business Mogul

1996 - Present

  • Founder of Snoop Dogg's Branding Empire, including cannabis ventures (Leafs by Snoop), entertainment (Snoopadelic Films), and gaming (Gangsta Gaming League).
  • Owner of Death Row Records, revitalizing one of hip-hop's most legendary labels.
  • Created Snoop Loopz, a cereal brand expanding into the food industry.
  • Successful partnerships with brands like Adidas, Corona, and Beyond Meat.

Actor, Media Personality & TV Host

1998 - Present

  • Hosted and starred in hit shows such as Martha & Snoop's Potluck Dinner Party, Coach Snoop, and Snoop Dogg's Father Hood.
  • Featured in major films including Training Day, Starsky & Hutch, and Soul Plane.
  • Voice acting roles in animated movies and video games (Call of Duty, The Addams Family).

Philanthropist & Community Leader

2005 - Present

  • Founded the Snoop Youth Football League, mentoring and supporting young athletes.
  • Advocates for criminal justice reform and education programs.
  • Regular donor to charities supporting underserved communities and social justice initiatives.
